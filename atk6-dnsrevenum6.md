Performs a fast reverse DNS enumeration and is able to cope with slow servers.
Examples:
  atk6-dnsrevenum6 dns.test.com 2001:db8:42a8::/48
  atk6-dnsrevenum6 dns.test.com 8.a.2.4.8.b.d.0.1.0.0.2.ip6.arpa
